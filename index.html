Left Otis. Went all-in on entrepreneurship

Entrepreneur, executive, and recognised expert in vertical transportation, smart mobility, and smart logistics. For 17 years I ran two careers in parallel — as CEO & Managing Director of Otis Greece & Cyprus, and as co-founder of iLINK, a telematics company I built from the ground up.

In September 2024 I went all-in on my own ventures. Today iLINK is a team of 25, serving fleet operators, logistics organisations, and public-sector clients across Greece — with recurring revenue above 85%, zero debt, and continuous growth through two major financial crises.

The combination of multinational discipline and entrepreneurial execution is the lens I apply to everything I build and advise. My background is engineering-first: NTUA, Carnegie Mellon, MBA, MIT Sloan. I prefer building the systems I lead.

Alongside iLINK, I co-founded Athenian Facility Services and BreathCenter, and I advise founders and executives on business strategy with a strong emphasis on service delivery.
